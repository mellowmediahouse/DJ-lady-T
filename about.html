Artist Biography

Born and raised in Matatiele (Bubesi Location), I started my journey as a resident DJ in Kokstad @ Mama Afrika. Known widely as a Gqom DJ for my electric, crowd-shaking sets.

I currently hold a residency @ Tahiti Lounge in Kokstad. I have performed at:

  • ANC Rally
  • Matatiele Bikers
  • Ntantes Festival
  • Laken Dam
  • Matatiele Music Festival

I’ve shared stages with national artists including Blag Diamond, CampMasters, Shenge Wasemhlankosi, DJ Dongs, DJ Tira, Amatycooler, Lindo Mthangayi, Duncan, Beast, DJ Answer, Jazzworkx and General Cmamane.

I recently produced a song titled Circles — available for downloads and streaming.
